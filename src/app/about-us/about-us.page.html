About Us About Us The Royal Insurance Corporation of Bhutan Limited (RICB) is the country's second oldest financial institute incorporated on 7th January 1975 under the Royal Charter of His Majesty the Fourth Druk Gyalpo Jigme Singye Wangchuck. RICB offers various services such as Life Insurance, General Insurance, Credit Facilities and other Social Security Schemes. Vision To be the premier financial service provider in the country and beyond, securing the ever evolving aspirations of the society. Mission We offer premier insurance, credit, and other social security services, delivering personalized services at affordable cost by professional employees through comprehensive network.
